Press <strong>Unlock/Lock</strong> to unlock and relock the query window. Before submitting, you are encouraged to first press <strong>Check Syntax</strong> to ensure your query is valid. Then, hit <strong>Submit</strong> to begin your query. Your query must only select "name, ra, dec" in that order like in the example query. Once finished, your results will be displayed in the column on the right and, if you check the <strong>Open in New Tab</strong> button, a new tab. At this point, you can <strong>Reset</strong> your results in preparation for another query.
